Health 360+ ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal data: name, email address
  • Health data: steps, sleep, heart rate, exercise, smoking/alcohol habits
  • Device data: data from smart devices synced with Health 360+

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Track your health progress and rewards
  • Improve user experience
  • Provide personalized guidance

3. Sharing of Data

  • We do not share your personal data with third parties without consent

4. Data Security

  • All data is encrypted and stored securely
  • We use SSL/TLS to protect data in transit
